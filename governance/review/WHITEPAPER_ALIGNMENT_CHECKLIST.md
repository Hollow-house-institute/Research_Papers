# Whitepaper Alignment Checklist

## Required Publication Governance Controls

The following concepts should be explicitly reflected where applicable across whitepapers, publication packages, interoperability sections, and governance crosswalks.

### Interoperability Boundary Language
- Use "illustrative interoperability mapping"
- Avoid "compliance mapping"
- Avoid "requirements equivalence"
- Avoid "certification mapping"

### Legal Boundary Language
Publications should clearly state:
- HHI supports governance assurance
- HHI supports operational traceability
- HHI supports runtime observability
- HHI does not independently determine legal compliance
- HHI does not replace legal review

### Governance Assurance vs Compliance Determination
Publications should distinguish:
- governance assurance
- operational evidence continuity
- compliance determination
- legal/regulatory adjudication

### Role Separation
Where governance obligations are discussed:
- distinguish provider obligations
- distinguish deployer obligations
- distinguish operational governance tooling

### Standards Precision
Use canonical titles:
- Article 9 — Risk Management System
- Article 12 — Record-Keeping
- Article 14 — Human Oversight

### Operational Positioning
Prefer:
- operational governance infrastructure
- governance observability
- replay-capable evidence
- runtime governance telemetry
- operational assurance infrastructure

Avoid:
- implied certification
- implied statutory equivalence
- implied guaranteed compliance

# Citation Appendix Normalization

All HHI publication packages should include normalized citation appendices using this structure.

## Citation Fields
- Source title
- Authority body or publisher
- Canonical URL or DOI
- Referenced article, section, control, or principle
- HHI operational mapping
- Mapping boundary

## Mapping Boundary Language
These references support interoperability, governance interpretation, and operational assurance. They do not create legal conclusions, certification claims, or statutory equivalence.

## Required Labels
Use: Illustrative interoperability mapping

Avoid:
- illustrative interoperability mapping
- illustrative interoperability mapping
- operational alignment
- satisfies Article
- guarantees compliance

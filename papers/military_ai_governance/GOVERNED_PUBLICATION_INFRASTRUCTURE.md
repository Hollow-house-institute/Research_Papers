# Governed Publication Infrastructure

This DOI package is structured as governed publication infrastructure, not only as a standalone paper.

## Included Governance Controls
- EU_AI_ACT_MAPPING_REVIEW_GUARDRAILS.md
- CANONICAL_STANDARDS_BIBLIOGRAPHY.md
- CITATION_APPENDIX_NORMALIZATION.md
- ROLE_SEPARATION_AND_LEGAL_BOUNDARY.md
- NOT_LEGAL_ADVICE_FOOTER.md
- WHITEPAPER_ALIGNMENT_CHECKLIST.md

## Function
These artifacts provide:
- governance metadata
- publication control infrastructure
- reviewer-boundary controls
- interoperability governance evidence
- legal and regulatory scope boundaries
- governance assurance vs compliance determination separation

## Boundary
This package supports operational governance assurance, traceability, and publication-review continuity. It does not independently determine legal compliance, certify regulatory conformity, or replace qualified legal review.
